This demo 

- records your webcam (using HTML5 APIs) and the webpage (using RRWeb library).
- webcam recordings are split in multiple segments (every 30s, configurable) and uploaded to the server
- webpage recording are uploaded at the end of the session
- the session ends after 45 seconds

Inspect the js console and network panel for more details.
